What is Shariah-compliant Financing?

Shariah-compliant financing, also known as Islamic financing, is a financial system that complies with Islamic law. Islamic finance frequently involves a contract between a lender and a borrower. The lender provides money for a specific purpose and the borrower repays the loan over a predetermined period. The agreement might also include a profit margin that is divided between the lender and borrower based on the success of the project or investment.
